About Rahul

Description
Details
Rahul Gandhi is an Indian politician, member of the Indian National Congress party, and scion of the Nehru-Gandhi family. He has held various positions within the party, focusing on poverty alleviation, rural development, and youth empowerment. His leadership abilities have drawn both support and criticism in Indian politics.

Full Name

Rahul Gandhi

Date of Birth

June 19, 1970

Political Affiliation

Indian National Congress (INC) party

Family Background

Son of former Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi and grandson of former Prime Minister Indira Gandhi
